Mirrors & Gallons by Joseph Awumee

Mirror and Gallons is a photograph that showcases the simplicity and elegance of everyday objects in an unexpected and thought-provoking way. The photograph captures two gallons of water placed on a mirror, with the reflection of the gallons and the surrounding environment creating a unique visual composition. The photograph, taken at an unknown location, highlights the beauty and versatility of everyday objects and the way they can be used to create art

The photograph is a simple composition, with two gallons of water placed on a mirror. However, the photograph is not just about the gallons, it is also about the reflection of the gallons and the surrounding environment. The reflection of the gallons creates a unique visual composition, with the lines and shapes of the gallons being repeated and distorted by the mirror. The reflection also creates a sense of depth and movement, as the viewer's eye is drawn from the foreground to the background.

The photograph also highlights the beauty of everyday objects. The gallons are not something that we would typically consider to be beautiful, but the photograph showcases their simplicity and elegance. The shape of the gallons, the smooth lines of the plastic, and the water inside, all come together to create a visually striking image. The photograph is a reminder that beauty can be found in unexpected places and that sometimes the simplest things can have the most impact.
